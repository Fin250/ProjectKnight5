◆ Women's Menstrual Cycle Awareness Circle ◆

Facilitated by: Lisa at Luna Moth

Course Length: 7 monthly sessions, May – December 2025 (plus optional taster)

Location & Time: Monthly Fridays · 7:00pm – 9:00pm · Worthing

About the Course

A face-to-face, intimate circle for women to deepen into menstruality and conscious menopause transition. Guided by Red School trained mentor Lisa, this space supports transformation, healing, and awareness. Each session includes guided meditation and embodied reflection.

This course is for women aged 20s–50s, cycling or in perimenopause/menopause, whether or not they have a womb. Ideal for those with cycle charting experience or who have attended intro sessions and wish to go deeper.

Course Commitment

You are asked to commit to at least 5 of the 7 core sessions. Payment is made upfront by selecting which dates you can attend and choosing your rate from a sliding scale:

  • £15 – community rate
  • £20 – standard rate
  • £25 – generous rate
Additional sessions can be topped up individually. Cash payments also accepted in person.
